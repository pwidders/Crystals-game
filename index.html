Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you add a specific amount of point to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if your total score goes abouve the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
